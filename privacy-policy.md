# Privacy Policy for Tessara

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Tessara operates the Tessara Bot application for Pinterest.

## Information Collection
We collect Pinterest account data solely for the purpose of creating and scheduling pins on behalf of the Tessara brand.

## Data Usage
All data collected through the Pinterest API is used exclusively for:
- Creating and publishing interior design inspiration pins
- Scheduling pin publication times

## Data Storage
We store minimal data required for the operation of our scheduling service. All data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties.
